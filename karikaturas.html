How it works?

Professionally drawn caricature is created from a photo sent to us;
Upon completion you will recieve a sample of the artwork along with a receipt;
In case of any required corrections, we will be happy to edit the work before finalizing;
Once you recieve the full caricature in JPG format, you will be able to print it on mugs, t-shirts, posters, etc.
As well as posting it on your favorite social network.

Advantages of a caricature:

A drawn caricature is a great way to mock your friends, family or co-workers;

In the process of drawing the caricature, all of your wishes, ideas and requirements will be listened to and taken into consideration;
It's possible to get your caricature within one day, if necessary. It couldn't be easier - do it all online;
Caricature is able to express a lot more than a regular photo that's forgotten somewhere in your hard drive, besides, the resolution of our caricatures is so detailed that they can be easily printed as large as 5 storey building without sacrificing any quality!
